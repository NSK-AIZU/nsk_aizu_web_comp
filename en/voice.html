Akishi Tada President

Why did you start NSK_AIZU?

Through working in agriculture, I learned that there are many farmlands that have lost their owners. As the population declines, so do connections between people. So instead of building something new, why not revive the places that were once used and turn them into spaces where people can connect? That's why I started NSK_AIZU.

ammannu Vice President

How has joining been?

I started getting involved through a cooking event. I haven't been able to join the farming activities much, but through these activities the community has grown, and meeting new people has been the best part for me.
